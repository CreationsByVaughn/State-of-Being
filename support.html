Support

If you need help with the State of Being app, please use one of the following options:

Email Support

Contact us at support@stateofbeingapp.com. We typically respond within 1–2 business days.

Common Questions

  • Where is my data stored? – All data is stored locally on your device.
  • How do I restore my purchase? – Use the “Restore Purchase” option in the app’s settings.
  • Do you share my information? – No, we do not collect or share personal data.

Feedback

We welcome your feedback and suggestions for improving the app. Please email us at support@stateofbeingapp.com.
